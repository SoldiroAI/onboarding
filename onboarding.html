Step 4 of 7 · Personality

How should Soldiro sound?

This shapes every email Claude writes — the first line, subject, and all 5 sequence steps. Pick one, then refine with custom instructions.

⚡
Direct & Sharp
No fluff. Short sentences. Respects the reader's time. Best for C-suite and founders.
🎯
Consultative
Insight-led. Demonstrates understanding of their world before the pitch. Best for VP/Director.
😊
Warm & Friendly
Conversational, human, approachable. Works well in lower-formality industries.
🔥
Challenger
Opens with a provocation or counterintuitive insight. High-risk, high-reward.
📊
Data-Driven
Leads with stats, benchmarks, and ROI language. Best for analytics-minded buyers.
📖
Story-Led
Opens with a customer narrative or "we worked with a company just like yours." Builds trust.
